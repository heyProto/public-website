We develop community, technology and capacity to enable a sustainable, scalable, replicable and equitable future for civic media.

 We collaborate to:
  • Help build connective tissue between the ecosystem's talent, institutions, resources and opportunities, to
  • Help build innovation sandboxes that experiment with storytelling, product, work and business models, to
  • Help build identity, influence, impact around underserved audiences, themes, languages and other verticals.

We are an events, software and consulting company for the development of media, and International Center for Journalists' (ICFJ) implementing partner in India.
