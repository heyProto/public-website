We are an events, technology and consulting company for the development of civic media.
In India, we are the implementing partner for the International Center for Journalists (ICFJ).
We are building community, software and services for development-focused media to:
  • Help build connective tissue between the ecosystem's talent, organisations, audiences and resources, to
  • Help build innovation sandboxes that experiment with editorial, product and business models, to
  • Help build identity, influence, impact around underserved audiences, themes and verticals.
